The epic tales of the Ten Thousand cells

Lobby. First, you stand in the lobby, a square containing 10×10 rooms with different shades of blue. In the lobby, click on a room to enter it.

Rooms. Each room contains 10×10 cells. Each cell is dark or light. When clicking on a dark cell, it transforms to a light cell. They is no way to do the opposite. Click on the circle to return to the lobby.

Minimap. Standing in a room, you can see your global position at the bottom left square. Furthermore, you can go to a neighbour room without going back to the lobby, by clicking the arrows on the control pad.

Artistic aim. The main goal is to draw awesome monochrome pixel pictures. Express yourself and draw letters, signs or geometric forms... Play alone or with friends: every player contributes at the same time on the same drawing!

Challenging aim. The secondary goal, more challenging, is to find one special cell among the Ten Thousand. This special cell ends the game and makes you choose the name of the whole draw!

Draws section. The finished artworks are therefore available in the draws section. There, admire the chefs-d’œuvre by clicking on arrows, and click on the circle to come back to the most recent picture.
